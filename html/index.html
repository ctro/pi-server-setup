About Juniper

I graduated from the Connecting Point School of Massage and Spa Therapies in Telluride, Colorado in 1996.

Shortly thereafter, I moved to Jackson Hole and began practicing massage therapy at Southern Touch Massage and at spas and hotels including Amangani, Spring Creek Ranch, and The Alpine House. In 1999, I started Three Peaks Massage and moved into my current downtown office.

I am nationally certified and a member of the American Massage Therapy Association. My style emphasizes sports stretching and deep tissue massage for the active recreationalist. I also offer hot stone therapy and reflexology treatments.

Each session reflects thought, attention to detail and professionalism. I feel lucky enough to have established a long standing clientele of repeat costumers for 16 years and counting. I enjoy spending my free time hiking and skiing and savoring all Jackson Hole has to offer.

Mission

To provide a unique massage experience that caters to each client's specific needs in a comfortable and nurturing environment, leaving them refreshed and revived.
